# Investor DM — Follow-up Sequence

Two universal templates + per-target hook lines. Use the Day +3 bump for all non-responders, then the Day +7 close-loop. Swap `{partner_name}`, `{fund_name}`, and `{fund_specific_hook}` before sending.

---

## Universal Day +3 bump

> Hi {partner_name} — bumping this in case it slipped. Happy to send a 1-page memo + 2-minute product walkthrough. {fund_specific_hook}. If this isn't a fit for {fund_name}, a quick pointer to whoever covers creator-AI or music infrastructure would be genuinely helpful.

## Universal Day +7 close-loop

> Hi {partner_name} — last touch for now. If the creator-owned AI music thesis isn't on {fund_name}'s current radar, totally fair. When it becomes active, **Lyrica 3 Pro / Empire 1 / Southern Lifestyle** is the shape I'd re-surface. Happy to share an updated traction snapshot and tight pilot structure then. Good building.

## Do / Don't on follow-ups

- **Do** send the Day +3 bump exactly 72 hours after the Day 0 DM (not before).
- **Do** attach or link the 1-page memo and the 2-minute walkthrough when the Day +3 mentions them — or remove the offer.
- **Don't** add a Day +5 touch between bump and close-loop. Two touches is the approved cadence.
- **Don't** re-send the close-loop if they read the Day +3. One close, then silent.
- **Don't** fabricate portfolio overlap or name-drop other investors.
