# Investor Handoff Email Template

Triggered when an investor contact replies "send me more" / "email me at X" / asks for the deck. Send within 2 hours of their reply. Attach the 1-page memo (PDF) and link the 2-minute walkthrough.

---

## Template

> **Subject:** Lyrica 3 Pro — 1-pager + 2-min product walkthrough
>
> Hi {first_name},
>
> Thanks for the reply. As promised: attached is the 1-page memo and the 2-minute product walkthrough link.
>
> **One-line:** Lyrica 3 Pro is creator-owned emotional AI music — human-feel output with derivative royalty routing — built on our own three-layer stack:
> - **Empire 1** — ownership + royalty rails (the infrastructure layer)
> - **Lyrica 3 Pro** — the flagship product (Sonance Pro + SL Universal)
> - **Southern Lifestyle** — built-in cultural and community distribution on day one
>
> Most companies in this space own one of those layers and rent the other two. We own all three, and the compounding across them is the defensibility case.
>
> **Attachments / links:**
> - `lyrica3pro_one_pager.pdf` _(attach before sending)_
> - 2-minute product walkthrough: {loom_or_video_url}
> - Product site: https://lyrica3.com
>
> **Asking for:** a 12-minute call this week or next with you or the right partner for creator-AI / music / infrastructure on your side. I'll keep it to exactly 12 minutes, screen-share included.
>
> Best,
> Manda
> Founder, Lyrica 3 Pro · Empire 1
> manda@empire1.cloud

---

## Pre-send checklist

- [ ] Fill `{first_name}` from their reply signature, not from the original DM target name (they may be routing to a different partner).
- [ ] Fill `{loom_or_video_url}` — do not send if the video is not yet recorded.
- [ ] Attach the PDF one-pager. Do not paste as inline markdown — this is an email, not a blog post.
- [ ] Double-check the subject line matches what you said you would send (keeps the thread searchable).
- [ ] Log the send in `investor_contact_tracker.md` → `intro_status = replied`, `next_step = await_meeting_booking_or_followup_day3`.

## Do NOT

- Add new product claims not present in the DM.
- Attach the full deck on the first handoff email — the 1-pager is enough to earn the 12-minute call.
- CC anyone else (legal, cofounder) on the first handoff — keep it 1:1 until the meeting is booked.
- Reply with new attachments if they don't respond within 7 days — use the Day +7 close-loop template instead.
