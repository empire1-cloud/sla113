# Original-art boundary

This pipeline is designed to generate original Empire-1 / SLA113 assets.

- Do not request copies of Fire Kirin, Juwa, or another operator's characters, cabinets, logos, UI, or proprietary artwork.
- Use those products only as broad gameplay-category references.
- Keep prompts focused on Empire-1's own Aztec-inspired fantasy design language.
- Preserve generation manifests and source contracts as provenance records.
- Confirm rights and cultural-review requirements before public or commercial release.
